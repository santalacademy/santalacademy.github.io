How to reach Santal Academy, Dumka

By Rail: Dumka is well connected by rail and road. It is well connected with other cities like New Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata, Ranchi, Patna, Varanasi and Bhubaneswar etc. of the country as well.
Santal Academy, Dumka is situated in Dighi, Dumka which is approximately 5 km away from the Dumka railway station.
