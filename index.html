Santal Academy

Santal Academy at the Sido-Kanhu Murmu University, Dumka, Jharkhand was established in 1996 but it had been dormant except the building itself which is being used as the office of the Vice-Chancellor as the university has not got her own buildings yet.

Mission & Vision

Santal Academy is a Unit of Sido Kanhu Murmu University, Dumka which is established in the year 1996. It aims at the sustainable, socio-economic and scientific development of santal community, by building a resilient community through research, innovation, training, capacity building, environmental impact assessment, database management, scientific social responsibility and technical interventions.
